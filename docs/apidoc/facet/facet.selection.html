facet.selection¶

Learner selection with hyperparameter optimization.

LearnerGrid encapsulates a PipelineDF and a grid of hyperparameters.

LearnerRanker selects the best pipeline and parametrization based on the pipeline and hyperparameter choices provided as a list of LearnerGrid.

Classes¶

LearnerEvaluation

A collection of scores for a specific parametrization of a learner pipeline, generated by a LearnerRanker.

LearnerGrid

A grid of hyper-parameters for tuning a learner pipeline.

LearnerRanker

Score and rank different parametrizations of one or more learners, using cross-validation.
